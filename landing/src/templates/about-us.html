Our Founding Investors

Combining the best of both worlds in Agriculture and Technology

ETG has emerged as one of Africa’s largest Agricultural Conglomerates. ETG’s footprint expands across sub-Saharan Africa, North America, Europe, the Middle East and South East Asian countries. ETG’s multinational operating entities are strategically located across 40 countries and are responsible inter alia for procurement, warehousing, processing and manufacturing of finished goods.

Techno Brain is one of the largest IT solutions company in Africa, recognized globally for t’s technology leadership and providing quality software services and products in Enterprise, ID, Tax and Finance domains. Africa 1st as it’s mission, a CMMi Level 5 company has operations in 21+ countries with 1200+ people providing innovative digital solutions to public and private sector organizations.

Manoj Shanker

Chairman, KwikBasket

In 20 years, Techno Brain has emerged as Africa’s leading Digital technology company Empowering Lives of our Customers using disruptive technology. As part of its journey we have launched KwikBasket.com with a vision to build Africa’s largest Data Platform.

KwikBasket’s mission is to bring Farmers and Customers together by using the power of Data. We strive to provide better price and access to our farmers which in turn reliable, fresh, properly stored Fruits & Vegetables to our Customers.

By 2040 world population will grow from 7 billion to 9 billion and there will be need for 70 to 100% increase in food production. We will be part of solution to this problem by leveraging habits of consumers and giving reliable data to Farmers to grow specific produce at a fair price in a sustainable way.

We are part of Digital Revolution in Agriculture Sector.
