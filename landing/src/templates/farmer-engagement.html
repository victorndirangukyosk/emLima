Farmer Engagement

Digital Agriculture is the use of new and advanced technologies and integrated systems, to enable farmers and other stakeholders within the agricultural value chain. We use Artificial Intelligence (AI) to help farmers determine the optimal date to sow crops, precisely allocate resources such as water, fertilizer, identify crop diseases for swifter treatment, and early detection. We also help farmers forecast crop seasons using historic production data, long-term weather forecasts, genetically modified seed information, and commodity pricing predictions. Financially, we support farmers by providing them with crop insurance plans and soft loans.
