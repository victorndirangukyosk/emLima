COVID-19 Precautionary Measures

Our customers' safety is our priority. To curb the spread of the Coronavirus, we have made all our deliveries contactless. All our personnel are regularly tested for the virus and prevention measures such as wearing of masks and sanitization are upheld at all our premises. We are also only taking electronic payments such as mobile money or bank transfers.

Stay Home, Stay Safe

Here's what we're doing on our end

Facility Operations
  1. Signs are posted on how to stop the spread of COVID-19 and promote everyday protective measures
  2. All areas of our food establishment, including restrooms and waiting areas are properly cleaned, stocked, sanitized and disinfected
  3. Ventilation systems including air ducts and vents in the facility are clean, free of mold, and operating properly
  4. Staff are properly trained on cleaning procedures to ensure safe and correct application of disinfectants
  5. A disinfection schedule or routine plan has been developed. We ensure sufficient stocks of cleaning and disinfecting supplies to accommodate ongoing cleaning and disinfection.
Employee Health / Screening
  1. We have a protocol to check employee health and personal hygiene practices within our establishment
  2. We are following the Ministry of Health's guidance and practices for employee health checks and screenings
  3. There is a plan to monitor and respond to a higher than normal level of absenteeism
  4. There is a policy for, and an adequate supply of, personal protective equipment (PPE) and/or cloth face coverings for our employees
  5. We have restricted the number of employees in shared spaces, including kitchens, break rooms, and offices to maintain at least a 6-foot distance between people
